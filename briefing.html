Buddy Checks

Plan your dive & dive your plan!

1 ) If your lost or seperated from your group, look for maximum 1 minute then ascent at a safe rate to the surface (without a safety stop) to reunite with your buddies.

2 ) Everyone should remain at the same depth as the dive leader.

3 ) Swim slow. The slower you go the more you see and the less air you will use.

4 ) Always stay close to your buddy! You may need their alternative air source (hopefully not).

5 ) Complete a safety stop (5 meters, 3 minutes) at the end of your dive. Ascend using a surface marker buoy (AKA, SMB or Safety Sausage).

Buddy Checks

    BCD

    • Inflates with LPI and orally
    • Deflates with hose and dump valves

    Weights

    • All weights secured
    • Understand quick release mechanisms
    • Right hand release

    Releases

    • All connected securely
    • Tank straps secured

    Air

    • Smells and tastes ok
    • Check SPG displays ok
    • Test second stage and alternative while watching SPG

    Final Check

    • Fins, masks and other equipment
    • Feeling comfortable and mentally ready to dive
